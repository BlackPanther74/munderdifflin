About Us

We are a fictious... excuse me, fictional paper company based in Scranton, PA. We were established in 2005 as an attempt to replicate the success of the British version of our show. We have gone on to have huge commercial and critical success until our main attraction decided to move on to other projects. We attempted to soldier on but only managed to keep the lights on for another season.
